Q&A

Where are you from?

I was born in San Francisco, California in 1993 and since then I've spent most of my life living in the East Bay (Oakland, Newark, San Leandro).

Why did you choose design?

To put it simply, in senior year of high school I was introduced to Photoshop and InDesign. And once I knew about them, I didn't want to stop using them.

What do you plan to accomplish with design?

The answer changes depending on when you ask me. But for the most part, I want to use design to tell stories.

What are your interests outside of design?

I like to listen to music and play video games (Zelda fan here). I love watching Korean and Japanese dramas, anime, telenovelas, documentaries and basically pretty much anything.
